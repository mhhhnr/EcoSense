Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

EcoSense respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the Platform.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: If you use the "My Location" feature, your browser shares your approximate coordinates with the Platform solely to fetch local air quality data. This data is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous, non-identifiable usage analytics may be collected to improve the Platform.
  • Search Queries: City and coordinate searches are transmitted to Open-Meteo and OpenStreetMap APIs to retrieve data.

Third-Party Services

EcoSense integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Open-Meteo Air Quality API — for real-time pollutant data
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — for geocoding and reverse geocoding
  • CartoDB — for map tile rendering
  • Google Fonts — for typography

Data Retention

EcoSense does not maintain a persistent user database. No personal data is collected or stored by the Platform.

Cookies

The Platform does not use tracking cookies. Session-level browser storage may be used to maintain UI state during your visit only.
